# Granny's lovely tacos

Ingredients:
 - A bit of veggies
 - A bit of black beans
 - Guacamole
 - Granny's secret sauce
 - A lot of love!
 - Tacos

Instructions:
 - Bring out that lovely smile!
 - Heat beans
 - Make guacamole
 - Add all ingredients on top of tacos
 - Enjoy with those you love
 - Clean the dishes, don't be rude to granny!
